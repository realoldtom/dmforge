Gust Of Wind 2 • Evocation
1 action Self

Create a 60-foot line of wind that pushes creatures 15 feet away on a failed save, slows movement toward you, disperses gas, and extinguishes unprotected flames. Can be redirected as a bonus action.

Thaumaturgy 0 • Transmutation
1 action 30 feet

Manifest a minor wonder within range: amplify voice, manipulate flames, cause ground tremors, create sound, open/close doors, change eye appearance. Maintain up to 3 effects simultaneously, dismissing as an action.
