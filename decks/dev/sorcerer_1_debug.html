Burning Hands 1 • Evocation
1 action Self

Shoot a 15-foot cone of flames from your hands, dealing 3d6 fire damage to creatures in its path. They can make a Dexterity saving throw for half damage. Flammable objects ignite in the area.

Charm Person 1 • Enchantment
1 action 30 feet

Charm Person: Charm a humanoid with a wisdom saving throw; they are charmed until spell ends or harmed, viewing you as a friendly acquaintance. They realize they were charmed when it ends.

Color Spray 1 • Illusion
1 action Self

Color Spray: Roll 6d10 to determine total hit points affected. Blinds creatures in a 15-foot cone based on ascending hit points until total hit points are depleted.

Comprehend Languages 1 • Divination
1 action Self

Comprehend Languages allows you to understand spoken and written languages for the duration, but does not decode secret messages or non-written glyphs. Reading text takes about 1 minute per page.

Detect Magic 1 • Divination
1 action Self

Detect Magic: Sense magic within 30 feet, see aura around magical creatures/objects, learn school of magic. Penetrates most barriers but blocked by stone, metal, lead, wood, or dirt.

Disguise Self 1 • Illusion
1 action Self

Disguise Self: Change appearance and clothing, but not body type. Illusion fails under physical inspection. Requires Investigation check to see through disguise.

Expeditious Retreat 1 • Transmutation
1 bonus action Self

This spell allows you to move at an incredible pace. When you cast this spell, and then as a bonus action on each of your turns until the spell ends, you can take the Dash action.

False Life 1 • Necromancy
1 action Self

Bolstering yourself with a necromantic facsimile of life, you gain 1d4 + 4 temporary hit points for the duration.

Feather Fall 1 • Transmutation
1 reaction 60 feet

Slow fall spell slows falling creatures' descent to 60 ft per round, preventing falling damage and allowing them to land safely on their feet if they touch the ground before the spell ends.

Fog Cloud 1 • Conjuration
1 action 120 feet

Create a 20ft radius sphere of heavily obscured fog at a point, lasting until wind disperses it or up to the spell's duration.

Jump 1 • Transmutation
1 action Touch

You touch a creature. The creature's jump distance is tripled until the spell ends.

Mage Armor 1 • Abjuration
1 action Touch

Mage Armor spell: Touch willing creature, grants AC of 13 + Dexterity mod, no armor. Ends if armor worn or dismissed.

Magic Missile 1 • Evocation
1 action 120 feet

Create three glowing force darts to strike creatures, dealing 1d4 + 1 force damage each, hitting simultaneously and target of your choice within range. Can direct darts to hit one or multiple creatures.

Shield 1 • Abjuration
1 reaction Self

An invisible barrier of magical force appears and protects you. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +5 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack, and you take no damage from magic missile.

Silent Image 1 • Illusion
1 action 60 feet

Create a visual illusion of an object or creature within 15ft cube that can move and change appearance. Interactions reveal it as an illusion, and a successful check allows seeing through it.
