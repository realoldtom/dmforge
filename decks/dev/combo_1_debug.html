Alarm 1 • Abjuration
1 minute 30 feet

Alarm spell protects a 20-foot area, alerting you mentally or audibly if creatures enter. Can exclude chosen creatures from triggering the alarm.

Animal Friendship 1 • Enchantment
1 action 30 feet

Charm a beast with less than 4 Intelligence by convincing it you mean no harm. If successful, it is charmed until harmed by you or your allies.

Charm Person 1 • Enchantment
1 action 30 feet

Charm Person: Charm humanoid, make Wis save. Advantage if fighting it. Charmed until spell ends or harm done. Regards as friend. Knows charmed after.

Comprehend Languages 1 • Divination
1 action Self

Comprehend Languages: Understand spoken and written languages for duration, touching text to read. Can't decode secret messages or arcane sigils.

Create Or Destroy Water 1 • Transmutation
1 action 30 feet

Create Water: Create 10 gallons of clean water in a container or as rain. Destroy Water: Eliminate 10 gallons of water in a container or fog in a 30-foot cube.

Cure Wounds 1 • Evocation
1 action Touch

A creature you touch regains a number of hit points equal to 1d8 + your spellcasting ability modifier. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

Detect Magic 1 • Divination
1 action Self

For a period of time, you detect magic within 30 feet. By using your action, you can see magical auras around visible creatures or objects, learning their school of magic. Barriers like stone, metal, lead, wood, or dirt can block the spell.

Detect Poison And Disease 1 • Divination
1 action Self

For a period of time, detect the presence and whereabouts of poisons, poisonous beings, and diseases within 30 feet. Identify the type of poison, creature, or disease. Can penetrate most obstacles, except specific materials.

Entangle 1 • Conjuration
1 action 90 feet

Entangling plants sprout in a 20-foot area, creating difficult terrain. Creatures must save or be restrained. They can try to break free with a Strength check. Plants disappear when the spell ends.

Expeditious Retreat 1 • Transmutation
1 bonus action Self

This spell allows you to move at an incredible pace. When you cast this spell, and then as a bonus action on each of your turns until the spell ends, you can take the Dash action.

Faerie Fire 1 • Evocation
1 action 60 feet

Choose a 20-ft cube area and objects/creatures glow in blue, green, or violet light, causing dim light. Attacks against them have advantage and they can't benefit from invisibility for the spell's duration if they fail a Dex save.

Fog Cloud 1 • Conjuration
1 action 120 feet

Create a 20-ft radius sphere of heavily obscured fog lasting until dispersed by moderate wind, centered on a point within range.

Goodberry 1 • Transmutation
1 action Touch

Create ten magical berries that can be eaten to restore 1 hit point each and sustain a creature for a day. Berries lose potency if not consumed within 24 hours.

Healing Word 1 • Evocation
1 bonus action 60 feet

A creature of your choice that you can see within range regains hit points equal to 1d4 + your spellcasting ability modifier. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

Hellish Rebuke 1 • Evocation
1 reaction 60 feet

You point your finger, and the creature that damaged you is momentarily surrounded by hellish flames. The creature must make a dexterity saving throw. It takes 2d10 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Hunter'S Mark 1 • Divination
1 bonus action 90 feet

Choose a target to mark as your quarry, dealing extra damage and gaining advantage on finding it. If it dies, you can mark a new target with a bonus action.

Illusory Script 1 • Illusion

"Magic Mouth" spell creates an illusion on written material to appear normal to designated creatures while appearing as unintelligible or a different message to others. Dispelled by dispel magic. Truesight reveals hidden message.

1 action 10 feet

All nonmagical food and drink within a 5-foot radius sphere centered on a point of your choice within range is purified and rendered free of poison and disease.
