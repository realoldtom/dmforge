Chill Touch 0 • Necromancy
1 action 120 feet

Create a spectral hand to attack a creature, dealing necrotic damage and preventing healing until your next turn. Undead targets have disadvantage on attacks against you. Damage increases with levels.

Entangle 1 • Conjuration
1 action 90 feet

Entangling plants sprout in a 20-ft square, creating difficult terrain. Creatures must save or be restrained. They can attempt to break free with a Strength check. Plants vanish when the spell ends.

Healing Word 1 • Evocation
1 bonus action 60 feet

A creature of your choice that you can see within range regains hit points equal to 1d4 + your spellcasting ability modifier. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.
