Disguise Self 1 • Illusion
1 action Self

Disguise Self: Change your appearance, height, weight, and clothes. Illusion doesn't hold to touch but resists casual observation. Can't alter body type.

Fire Bolt 0 • Evocation
1 action 120 feet

Hurl a mote of fire at a target, making a ranged spell attack. Deals 1d10 fire damage, ignites flammable objects. Damage increases with levels: 2d10 at 5th level, 3d10 at 11th, and 4d10 at 17

Mage Hand 0 • Conjuration
1 action 30 feet

Create a spectral hand within 30 feet, lasting until dismissed. Control hand to manipulate objects, open doors, or pour liquids, within 30 feet of you and not exceeding 10 pounds in weight.

True Strike 0 • Divination
1 action 30 feet

You extend your hand and point a finger at a target in range. Your magic grants you a brief insight into the target's defenses. On your next turn, you gain advantage on your first attack roll against the target, provided that this spell hasn't ended.
