Eldritch Blast 0 • Evocation
1 action 120 feet

Eldritch Blast shoots a beam of energy at a target within range, dealing 1d10 force damage on hit. At higher levels, it shoots multiple beams, with up to four beams at 17th level, each requiring a separate attack roll.

Find Familiar 1 • Conjuration
1 hour 10 feet

Summon a celestial, fey, or fiend familiar in animal form (bat, cat, crab, etc.) that obeys your commands, acts independently in combat, communicates telepathically, and can deliver touch spells for you within 100 feet.

Minor Illusion 0 • Illusion
1 action 30 feet

Create a lasting sound or image within range, ending when dismissed or recast. Sounds range from whispers to screams, while images can't create senses. Interacting reveals the illusion, and creatures can discern it with a successful check.
